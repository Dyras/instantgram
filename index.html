[instantgram]

[instantgram] is a bookmarklet with purpose of download instagram images. Tiny, simple, without necessity of extensions or downloads. Just drag the [instantgram] link to bookmark bar of your browser, navigate to the instagram.com (web), open a instagram post (photo) and click at bookmarklet. Just works.
